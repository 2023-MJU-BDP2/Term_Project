(브레이크뉴스 경기동북부)하인규 기자=서울특별시의회 김형재 의원(국민의힘, 강남2)은 지난 11월8일 제321회 정례회 도시안전건설위원회 소관 서울시 도시기반시설본부 행정감사에서 서울시에서 시행중인 대형해체공사장에 대한 근로자 안전교육, 공사장 주변 건물 및 보행자 안전확보 등 안전관리 강화를 주문했다고 보도자료를 통해 밝혔다.

김 의원은 지난 2021년 광주 철거건물 붕괴사고로 인해 안전 감독 강화의 필요성이 대두되었다며, 해체공사장에 대한 안전점검을 확대해야 한다고 지적했다. 또한, 전국적으로 해체공사장에서 안전사고가 재발하고 있어 이에 대한 대책이 필요하다고 언급했다.

최근에는 지난 8월 수원에서 건물 해체공사장 외부 패널을 해체하던 근로자 1명이 추락하여 사망하는 사고와 8월 강남 르메르디앙호텔 철거공사장에서 철거 중 화재가 발생하였고, 지난 10월 30일 종로경찰서 철거 현장 붕괴, 11월 6일 마포구 동교동 리모델링 건물 공사 가림막이 쓰러져 2명의 인명피해가 발생했다며 우려를 표명했다.

김 의원은 “도시기반시설본부 발주공사 중 해체공사를 포함하는 사업은 소규모 해체공사도 수시 점검하여 안전사고를 예방해야 하며, 특히 지난 7월 서울시에서 배포한 ‘건축물 해체공사 안전관리 매뉴얼’을 더욱 강화해야 한다”고 강조했다.

또한 김 의원은 “해체공사장 내 안전사고 예방을 위해서 현장작업자, 관리자에 대한 철거공사장 안전교육을 철저히 해야 한다”며 “공사장 내 안전사고뿐만 아니라 공사장 주변에도 사고 발생 위험성이 매우 높은 공사이므로 공사장 주변 건물이나 주변 보행자 안전 확보를 위한 방안도 마련해야 한다”고 촉구했다.

마지막으로 김 의원은 “앞으로도 시민, 작업자의 안전을 위해 선제적이고 정기적인 점검을 실시하고, 안전사고가 발생하지 않도록 안전관리를 강화해야 한다”고 주문했다.

Seoul City Council member Kim Hyeong-jae urges expansion of safety inspections at large-scale demolition construction sites in Seoul.

- Rep. Kim ordered strengthening of the ‘Building Dismantling Construction Safety Management Manual’, including safety training for workers at demolition construction sites in Seoul and securing the safety of buildings and pedestrians around construction sites.

-Reporter Ha In-gyu

(Break News Northeast Gyeonggi) = Seoul Metropolitan Council member Kim Hyeong-jae (People Power Party, Gangnam 2) said at the 321st regular session of the Urban Safety Construction Committee on November 8 that he inspected the large-scale demolition construction site being carried out in Seoul during an administrative audit of the Seoul Metropolitan Government's Urban Infrastructure Headquarters. It was announced in a press release that it was ordered to strengthen safety management, including safety training for workers and ensuring the safety of buildings and pedestrians around construction sites.

Representative Kim said that the need to strengthen safety supervision has emerged due to the collapse of a demolition building in Gwangju in 2021, and he pointed out that safety inspections at demolition construction sites should be expanded. He also noted that safety accidents are recurring at demolition construction sites across the country and that measures need to be taken to address them.

Recently, a worker fell to his death while dismantling external panels at a building demolition site in Suwon last August, a fire broke out during demolition at the Le Meridien Hotel demolition site in Gangnam in August, and the Jongno Police Station demolition site collapsed on October 30. , expressed concern that on November 6, a construction screen for a remodeling building in Donggyo-dong, Mapo-gu collapsed, resulting in two casualties.

Representative Kim said, “Projects that include dismantling work among the projects ordered by the Urban Infrastructure Headquarters must regularly inspect even small-scale dismantling work to prevent safety accidents. In particular, the ‘Building Dismantling Construction Safety Management Manual’ distributed by the Seoul Metropolitan Government in July of last year should be further reviewed. “It needs to be strengthened,” he emphasized.

In addition, Rep. Kim said, “In order to prevent safety accidents at the demolition construction site, we must thoroughly provide safety training at the demolition construction site for site workers and managers.” He added, “This is a construction project where the risk of accidents occurring not only within the construction site but also around the construction site is very high, so buildings around the construction site are very important.” He urged, “We must also come up with a plan to ensure the safety of pedestrians in the area.”

Lastly, Rep. Kim ordered, “We must continue to conduct preemptive and regular inspections to ensure the safety of citizens and workers, and strengthen safety management to prevent safety accidents from occurring.”
