▲ 여수시가 1일부터 2026년 12월 31일까지 3년간 행정안전부 주관 ‘국민행복민원실’ 재 인증 우수기관으로 선정됐다.







행정안전부가 주관하는 '국민행복민원실' 인증 우수기관에 여수시가 재선정됐다.

1일 여수시에 따르면 '국민행복민원실'은 행안부가 전국 지자체와 교육청, 세무서, 경찰서 등 공공기관을 대상으로 민원실 운영 우수기관을 선정하고 인증하는 제도다.

이에 따라 민원실 내·외부 환경과 민원서비스 종합평가 고객 만족도조사 등 서면심사와 현지실사를 통해 결정된다.

앞서 여수시는 지난 2020년 최초 인증 이후 연이은 재 인증으로 이날부터 오는 2026년 12월 31일까지 3년간 연장된다.

여수시는 ▲화요야간 행복민원실 운영 ▲무인민원발급기 확대 운영 등을 통해 수요자 중심의 행정 서비스를 제공했으며 ▲본 청사 로비와 주차장 환경개선 ▲석면 건축자재 정비 ▲웨어러블 캠, 휴대용 녹음기록장치 등 휴대용 보호장비 도입 ▲민원창구 투명 안전가림막 설치 등 시민과 직원 모두가 안전한 민원환경 조성에 집중해왔다.

더불어 민원처리 공간과 분리된 카페형 분위기의 고객쉼터 마련, 친환경 실내조경 설치, 건강관리코너, 북카페 등 다양한 테마 공간을 조성하고 고객 도움벨, 민원실 내 버스정보안내기 설치 등 시민들의 편의 증진을 위해 힘써왔다.

이번 재 인증에 따라 시는 1일 정기명 시장과 관계공무원이 참석한 가운데 국민행복민원실 현판식을 갖고 시민중심 시정구현을 다시금 다짐했다.

최윤모 민원지적과장은 "'아름다운 여수 행복한 시민’이라는 시민중심의 시정방침에 따라 모든 시민들이 민원실을 편하게 이용할 수 있도록 노력해 왔다"며 "양질의 민원서비스 구현에 최선을 다하겠다"고 밝혔다.

Ministry of Public Administration and Security selects Yeosu City’s ‘People’s Happiness Civil Service Office’ as an excellent certification agency Creation of a customer-centered civil affairs office environment and re-certification through evaluation of customer satisfaction civil service services reporter goYongbae

Yeosu City was re-selected as an excellent organization certified by the ‘People’s Happiness Civil Service Office’ hosted by the Ministry of Public Administration and Security.

According to Yeosu City on the 1st, the ‘People’s Happiness Civil Service Office’ is a system in which the Ministry of Public Administration and Security selects and certifies excellent organizations in operating the civil service office for public institutions such as local governments, offices of education, tax offices, and police stations across the country.

Accordingly, the decision is made through written review and on-site inspection, including the internal and external environments of the civil affairs office, comprehensive evaluation of civil affairs services, and customer satisfaction surveys.

Previously, Yeosu City has been re-certified continuously since its first certification in 2020, and it will be extended for three years from this day until December 31, 2026.

Yeosu City provided consumer-centered administrative services through ▲operating the Happy Civil Service Center on Tuesday nights ▲expanding the operation of unmanned civil service issuance machines, ▲improving the environment of the main building lobby and parking lot ▲maintaining asbestos building materials ▲portable protective equipment such as wearable cams and portable recording devices. Introduction ▲We have focused on creating a safe civil complaint environment for both citizens and employees, such as installing transparent safety barriers at the complaint window.

In addition, we created a customer rest area with a cafe-like atmosphere separate from the civil service area, installed eco-friendly indoor landscaping, created various themed spaces such as a health management corner, and a book cafe, and installed a customer help bell and a bus information guide in the civil service office to improve convenience for citizens. I've been working hard.

Following this re-certification, the city held a signboard hanging ceremony for the People's Happiness Civil Service Office on the 1st, attended by Mayor Jeong Ki-myeong and relevant public officials, and once again pledged to implement citizen-centered city administration.

Choi Yun-mo, head of the civil affairs department, said, “We have been working to ensure that all citizens can comfortably use the civil affairs office in accordance with the citizen-centered municipal policy of ‘Beautiful Yeosu Happy Citizens.’ We will do our best to implement high-quality civil affairs services.”
