Danyang Police Station, autonomous police security council life safety division regular meeting held

-im changyong reporter

Danyang Police Station held a regular meeting of the Life Safety Division of the Autonomous Police Security Council on the 21st to promote regional customized security policies.

At the meeting on this day, various agenda items for promoting regional customized security policies were discussed, including explanation of the operating regulations of the Autonomous Police Security Council, explanation of major tasks for the past 23 years, factors of residents' anxiety and improvement plans.

Chief Park Hee-gyu said, “We will do our best to reflect residents’ opinions in security policies, actively communicate with them, and create a safe Danyang where residents can feel at ease.”
