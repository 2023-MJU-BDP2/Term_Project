【충북 브레이크뉴스】임창용 기자=충북경찰청(청장 정상진)은, 연말연시 각종 모임 등 잦은 술자리로 인한 음주운전 증가 우려에 따라 오는 11월 20일부터 내년 1월말까지 집중적인 음주운전 단속을 추진한다고 밝혔다.

올해 충북 도내 음주운전 교통사고는 그간의 경찰의 음주운전 근절활동과 코로나19로 인한 음주 문화의 변화로 점증적으로 감소한 것으로 나타났다.

지난 3년간 연말연시 음주운전에 의한 교통사고는 소폭 증가하였고 연말연시 교통사고 중 절반 이간대에 발생되고 있어 취약시간대 단속활동을 더욱 강화한다는 방침이다.

이번 연말연시 음주운전 특별단속 기간 중 경찰은, 지역별 유흥가 등 음주운전 위험구간을 중심으로 수시 장소를 변경하며 단속 활동을 추진하는 한편, 도경찰청 주관으로 교통기동대, 교통경찰 등단속 경력을 최대 투입한 도내 일제단속과 경찰서 자체 단속을 한층 강화할 예정이다.

택시및 화물차 등 사업용 차량과 개인형 이동장치에 대해서도 예외없이 음주운전 단속을 실시할 계획이다.

경찰은, 각종 모임이 많아지는 연말연시 음주운전에 대해서는 엄중한 단속을 추진할 예정으로, 음주시에는 필히 대중교통을 이용하는 등 안전한 교통문화 정착에 동참하여 줄 것을 당부했다.

Chungbuk police promote intensive crackdown on drunk driving during the year-end and New Year holidays

Special crackdown from November 20th to January next year

-im changyong reporter

The Chungbuk Police Agency (Chief Commissioner Jeong Sang-jin) announced that it will pursue an intensive crackdown on drunk driving from November 20 to the end of January next year due to concerns about an increase in drunk driving due to frequent drinking parties such as various gatherings during the year-end and New Year holidays.

This year, traffic accidents caused by drunk driving in North Chungcheong Province were found to have gradually decreased due to police efforts to eradicate drunk driving and changes in drinking culture due to COVID-19.

Over the past three years, traffic accidents caused by drunk driving during the year-end and New Year holidays have increased slightly, and half of the traffic accidents occurring during the year-end and New Year holidays occur during this time zone, so the plan is to further strengthen crackdown activities during vulnerable times.

During this year-end and New Year's special crackdown on drunk driving, the police will frequently change locations and promote crackdown activities focusing on drunk driving risk areas such as regional entertainment districts, while maximizing crackdown experience from traffic riot squads and traffic police under the supervision of the Provincial Police Agency. We plan to further strengthen local crackdowns and police stations' own crackdowns.

We plan to crack down on drunk driving without exception for business vehicles such as taxis and trucks, as well as personal mobility devices.

The police plan to strictly crack down on drunk driving during the year-end and New Year holidays, when various gatherings increase, and urged people to participate in establishing a safe transportation culture by making sure to use public transportation when drinking.
