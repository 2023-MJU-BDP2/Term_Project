【충북 브레이크뉴스】임창용 기자=제69대 단양경찰서장으로 취임한 박희규 총경이 취임 100일을 맞았다.

박희규 서장은 ‘빈틈없는 치안, 안전한 단양, 행복한 단양’의 구현을 목표로 취임 후 여러 치안활동을 펼쳤다.

지난 8월 8일 세계스카우트잼버리대회 참가 대원들의 안전관리를 위한 치안대책을 수립하여 대원들의 안전을 위해 치안 활동을 진행했다.

집중호우로 인한 피해를 예방하기 위해 침수 취약지를 파악하여 선제적 예방 치안 활동도 전개했다.

또한, 한마음 자율방범대 체육대회에 참가하는 등 지역주민들과 소통을 위한 창구를 마련하여 민·경 협력 치안 구축에 노력을 기울였다.

박희규 서장은 “초심물망의 자세로 처음 맡은바 단양군의 안전을 지키고 군민을 보호하겠다는 소임을 마음에 다시 새기고, 군민과 소통하며 군민의 기대에 부응할 수 있는 치안서비스 제공에 최선을 다하겠다”라고 전했다.

Park Hee-gyu, the 69th Chief of Danyang Police Station, celebrates his 100th day in office.

-im changyong reporter

Superintendent Park Hee-gyu, who was inaugurated as the 69th Chief of Danyang Police Station, celebrated his 100th day in office.

Chief Park Hee-gyu has carried out various public security activities since taking office with the goal of realizing ‘plenteous security, safe Danyang, and happy Danyang.’

On August 8th, security measures were established to ensure the safety of participants in the World Scout Jamboree and security activities were carried out to ensure the safety of the members.

To prevent damage from torrential rain, he identified flood-prone areas and carried out preemptive preventive security activities.

In addition, efforts were made to establish public-police cooperation and public security by establishing channels for communication with local residents, such as participating in the Hanmaeum Autonomous Crime Prevention Team sports competition.

Chief Park Hee-gyu said, “I will keep in mind the mission of protecting the safety of Danyang-gun and protecting its citizens, which I took on for the first time with a beginner’s attitude, and I will do my best to communicate with the residents and provide security services that can meet their expectations.”
