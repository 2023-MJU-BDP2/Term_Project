



(무안=브레이크뉴스) 이학수 기자=전남도자치경찰위원회는 지난 20일 정기회의를 개최하고 2023년 무안· 강진·진도경찰서 자치경찰사무 종합감사 결과보고(안) 등 9건의 안건을 보고 및 심의·의결했다.(사진)

이번 자치경찰 종합감사 결과 보고에는 지난 9월~10월 감사를 실시했던 무안·강진·진도경찰서를 대상으로 범죄예방 환경조성, 아동안전지킴 선발·관리, 교통안전시설물 설치·관리 등 위원회 추진 시책을 중점 점검해 시정·주의 등 18건의 처분 요구사항을 심의·의결했다.

이날 심의에선 어린이 보호구역 내 승·하차 구간 설치 위치가 부적절해 불법 정차하고 있는 사례를 지적하는 등 현장 중심의 감사에 집중했다. 또 범죄예방 시설을 설치할 수 없는 장기방치 사유지에 대해 시설물을 설치할 수 있도록 지원 조례를 개정하는 등 지방자치단체와 협업해 문제를 해결한 우수사례를 발굴·전파했다.

또한 지난달 경찰청 조직개편으로 범죄예방·대응에 특화된 기동순찰대 운영을 위한 ‘전남 광역 기동순찰대 운영 계획’에 대해 보고받았다. 계획에 대해서는 동·서부권 2개 권역으로 운영할 예정이었으나 전남지역의 특성에 맞춰 탄력적으로 운영할 것을 주문했다.

이외에도 회의에선 2023년 2학기 아동·청소년 안전확보 집중 활동 결과, 이륜차 폭주행위 근절 및 예방대책, 2023년 교통사망사고 분석 및 대책 등의 보고 안건에 대해 심도 있게 검토했다.

조만형 위원장은 “전남 자치경찰 시책의 실효성 확보에 중점을 두고 각종 정책이 지역 특성에 맞게 도민 편익을 위해 추진됐는지를 중점적으로 감사했다”며 “내년도에도 일하는 분위기 조성에 힘쓰고, 자치경찰위원회의 사업예산이 적절히 쓰이고 있는지 별도 감사도 실시할 예정이다”고 말했다.

Jeonnam Autonomous Police Committee, focused inspection of front-line autonomous police policies

At the regular meeting, 9 matters were deliberated and resolved, including a report on the results of a comprehensive audit of autonomous police affairs... Discovering best cases of local government collaboration solutions, such as field-oriented audits and ordinance revisions

(Muan = Break News) Reporter Haksu Lee = The Jeonnam Provincial Autonomous Police Committee held a regular meeting on the 20th and reported, deliberated, and decided on 9 agenda items, including a report (draft) on the results of a comprehensive audit of autonomous police affairs at Muan, Gangjin, and Jindo Police Stations in 2023. (photo)

This report on the results of the comprehensive audit of autonomous police includes policies promoted by the committee, such as creating a crime prevention environment, selecting and managing child safety guards, and installing and managing traffic safety facilities, targeting the Muan, Gangjin, and Jindo police stations that were audited from September to October last year. Through intensive inspection, 18 disposal requests, including corrections and cautions, were reviewed and resolved.

The deliberation on this day focused on field-oriented audits, such as pointing out cases of illegal stopping due to inappropriate installation locations for boarding and disembarking sections in child protection areas. In addition, we collaborated with local governments to discover and disseminate excellent cases of problem solving, such as revising support ordinances to allow installation of facilities on long-term neglected private land where crime prevention facilities cannot be installed.

In addition, last month, with the reorganization of the National Police Agency, we were briefed on the ‘Jeonnam Metropolitan Area Mobile Patrol Operation Plan’ to operate a mobile patrol unit specialized in crime prevention and response. Regarding the plan, it was planned to be operated in two regions, the eastern and western regions, but it was requested to operate flexibly in accordance with the characteristics of the Jeollanam-do region.

In addition, the meeting reviewed in-depth reporting agenda such as the results of intensive activities to ensure the safety of children and adolescents in the second semester of 2023, eradication and prevention measures for two-wheeled vehicle driving, and analysis and measures for traffic fatal accidents in 2023.

Chairman Cho Man-hyeong said, “We focused on ensuring the effectiveness of Jeonnam autonomous police policies and focused on auditing whether various policies were promoted for the benefit of residents in accordance with regional characteristics.” He added, “We will strive to create a working atmosphere next year as well, and review the autonomous police committee’s business budget.” “We plan to conduct a separate audit to ensure it is being used appropriately,” he said.
