【브레이크뉴스 포항】박영재 기자=포항시 남구 호미곶 앞 해상에서 어선간 충돌로 선원 1명이 부상당해 병원으로 긴급 이송됐다.

▲ A호 선원 이송 (C) 포항해양경찰서



10일 포항해양경찰서(서장 성대훈)에 따르면 전날인 9일 오후 4시 43분쯤 포항시 남구 호미곶 북동 6.4km(3.5해리)에서 어선 A호(3톤급, 승선원 2명)와 어선 B호(9톤급, 승선원 4명)가 충돌했다는 신고를 접수했다.

이에 호미곶파출소는 현장 이동 중 A호, B호 선장에게 연락해 선박, 선원의 안전상태를 확인했다.

A호는 충돌로선원 C씨(여, 60대)가 넘어져 허리와 다리에 통증이 있었고 조타기가 작동되지 않는 상태였다. B호는 선원, 선박 모두 이상이 없었다.

호미곶파출소 연안구조정이 현장에 먼저 도착했지만 파고가 높아 계류가 쉽지 않았다. 이후 P-11정이 도착해 C씨를 태웠다. 하지만 P-11정은 호미곶항안으로 진입이 곤란해 다시금 호미곶파출소 연안구조정을 이용해 호미곶항으로 이송해 119에 인계했다.

A호는 민간해양구조선 D호(3톤급, 승선원 2명)의 도움으로 호미곶항으로 안전하게 예인되어 입항했다. 포항해양경찰서는 자세한 충돌경위에 대해 조사를 진행할 예정이다.

성대훈 서장은 “조업선 간에 발생하는 경미한 충돌은 충분히 예방이 가능하다. 충돌회피 동작을 확실히 해줄것”을 당부했다. 아울러 종합상황실, 각 파출소, 포항항VTS에는 사고예방을 위한 더욱 철저한 모니터링을 하도록 주문했다.

Collision between fishing boats in front of Homigot, Pohang, one injured person urgently transported

One sailor was injured in a collision between fishing boats at sea in front of Homigot, Nam-gu, Pohang City and was rushed to the hospital.

According to the Pohang Marine Police Station (Chief Seong Dae-hoon) on the 10th, at approximately 4:43 p.m. on the 9th, the previous day, fishing boat A (3-ton class, 2 crew members) and fishing boat B (9-ton class) were caught 6.4 km (3.5 nautical miles) northeast of Homigot, Nam-gu, Pohang-si. , 4 crew members) received a report of a collision.

Accordingly, Homigot Police Station contacted the captains of A and B while moving to the scene to check the safety of the vessel and crew.

Crew member C (female, in her 60s) fell due to a collision with ship A, suffered pain in her back and legs, and the steering wheel did not work. In B, there were no problems with both the crew and the ship.

The coastal rescue boat from Homigot Police Station arrived at the scene first, but mooring was not easy due to the high waves. Afterwards, a P-11 arrived and picked up Mr. C. However, it was difficult for the P-11 boat to enter Homigot Port, so it was transported to Homigot Port again using the Homigot Police Station coastal rescue boat and handed over to 119.

Ship A was safely towed and entered port at Homigot Port with the help of private marine rescue vessel D (3-ton class, 2 crew members).

Pohang Marine Police Station plans to conduct a detailed investigation into the circumstances of the collision.

Chief Seong Dae-hoon said, “Minor collisions that occur between fishing vessels are fully preventable. He urged, “Be sure to take collision avoidance actions.”

In addition, the General Situation Room, each police station, and Pohang Port VTS were ordered to conduct more thorough monitoring to prevent accidents.
