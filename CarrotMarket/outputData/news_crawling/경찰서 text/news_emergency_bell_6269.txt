▲ 보성군 지난 2일 다향아트밸리에서 2023 재난대응 안전한국훈련을 실시했다.





(보성=브레이크뉴스) 이학수 기자=전남 보성군은 지난 2일 대형 산불 재난에 신속히 대처하고 대응체계 확립을 위해 군민과 함께하는 ‘2023 재난대응 안전한국훈련’을 실시했다고 밝혔다.

재난대응 안전한국훈련은 범국가적 재난 대비 태세 강화를 목적으로 매년 실시하고 있으며, 중앙부처와 시·도, 지자체, 공공기관 등 309개 기관이 참여한다.

이번 훈련은 지역 내 재난 발생 위험도 분석에 따라 재난 발생 가능성이 높은 산불재난을 훈련유형으로 선정했으며, △보성군, △보성소방서, △보성경찰서, △한국전력보성지사, △KT보성지점, △보성군 의용소방대, △보성군 자율방재단, △국민체험단 등 200여 명이 참석했다.

특히, 행정안전부 기본계획에 따라 산불 현장과 재난안전대책본부를 실시간으로 연계해 진행하는 통합 연계 훈련이 처음으로 도입됐으며, 현장에 산불 진화 헬기 1대를 비롯한 의용소방대, 자율방재단 100여 명이 출동하는 등 실제상황을 방불케 하는 훈련이 진행됐다.

김규웅 보성군 부군수는 “가을철 산불 조심 기간을 맞아 대형 산불에 대응하기 위해 군민이 참여하는 실전 중심의 훈련을 실시했다.”라며, “훈련을 통해 유관기관과 협업체계를 확립하고 군민의 소중한 생명과 재산을 보호하는데 만전을 기하겠다.”라고 말했다.

한편, 보성군은 2022 재난대응 안전한국훈련 평가에서 우수기관 선정 및 행정안전부 장관 표창을 수상했으며, 5년 연속 재난관리평가 우수기관으로 선정되는 등 지역안전도 향상에 크게 기여했다.

Boseong-gun conducts 2023 disaster response safe country training

The military establishes a collaboration system with related agencies and groups to respond to large-scale forest fires.

(Boseong = Break News) Reporter Haksu Lee = Boseong-gun, Jeollanam-do announced on the 2nd that it conducted the ‘2023 Disaster Response Safe Nation Training’ with residents to quickly respond to large-scale forest fire disasters and establish a response system.

The Safe Nation Disaster Response Training is conducted every year for the purpose of strengthening national disaster preparedness, and 309 organizations, including central ministries, cities, provinces, local governments, and public institutions, participate.

For this training, forest fire disasters, which are highly likely to occur, were selected as the training type according to the analysis of the risk of disaster occurrence in the region, and △Boseong-gun, △Boseong Fire Department, △Boseong Police Station, △Korea Electric Power Corporation Boseong Branch, △KT Boseong Branch, and △Boseong-gun Volunteer Service. About 200 people, including the fire brigade, Boseong-gun Autonomous Defense Foundation, and the National Experience Group, attended.

In particular, in accordance with the basic plan of the Ministry of Public Administration and Security, integrated training that links the forest fire site and the Disaster and Safety Countermeasures Headquarters in real time was introduced for the first time, and about 100 people from the volunteer fire department and autonomous firefighting foundation, including one forest fire extinguishing helicopter, are dispatched to the site. Training was conducted reminiscent of real situations.

Kim Gyu-woong, Deputy Governor of Boseong-gun, said, “We conducted practical training with the participation of residents to respond to large-scale forest fires during the fall forest fire caution period.” He added, “Through the training, we established a collaboration system with related organizations and the precious lives and property of residents.” “We will do our best to protect,” he said.

Meanwhile, Boseong-gun was selected as an excellent organization and received a commendation from the Minister of Public Administration and Security in the 2022 disaster response safe country training evaluation, and contributed greatly to improving local safety by being selected as an excellent organization in disaster management evaluation for five consecutive years.
