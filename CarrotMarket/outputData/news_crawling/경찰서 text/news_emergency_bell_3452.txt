Danyang Police Station conducts joint patrol to lead youth after completing college entrance exams

-im changyong reporter

Danyang Police Station (Chief Park Hee-gyu) conducted joint patrols at night with police station partner organizations on the 16th, the day of the College Scholastic Ability Test.

On this day, about 50 people from the police station, autonomous crime prevention unit, and life safety council attended the joint patrol and patrolled the Danyang-eup entertainment district and Gugyeong Market area to prevent and promote deviant behavior such as drinking and smoking by teenagers who were freed from tension after the college entrance exam.

In addition, after the college entrance exam, they announced that they plan to educate business owners and employees on compliance measures such as restricting youth entry and verifying ID cards, focusing on businesses harmful to youth.

Police Chief Park Hee-gyu said, “We will protect youth from harmful environments and conduct periodic prevention and inspection to prevent accidents resulting from this.”
