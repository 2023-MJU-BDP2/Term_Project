▲ 만수6동 골마루근린공원 새단장(사진제공:남동구) (C) 박상도 기자

(브레이크뉴스인천 박상도 기자) 남동구가 30일 만수6동 골마루근린공원을 어린이부터 어르신까지 주민 모두가 즐기고 이용할 수 있는 휴식 공간으로 새단장했다고 밝혔다.

남동구 따르면, 골마루근린공원은 담방마을아파트와 담방초등학교에 인접해 주민들이 즐겨 찾는 휴식 공간이지만, 시설 노후가 심하고 장마 때 배수가 되지 않는 등 안전사고 위험이 있어 전면 시설 정비가 필요한 상황이었다.

이에 구는 원도심 노후 공원 환경개선 사업의 일환으로 7억 원의 예산을 확보, 올해 3월 주민설명회를 거쳐 실시설계 용역 완료 후 최근 공사를 완료했다.

또, 구는 공원이 위치한 만수3지구에 공동주택이 많고 다양한 연령층이 거주하는 지역 특색을 고려해 사업을 추진했다.

우선 노년층의 선호도가 높은 운동기구와 젊은 성인들을 위한 최신식 맨몸운동기구인 스트릿파크를 조성했다.

아울러, 에너지 넘치는 아이들이 맘껏 뛰놀 수 있는 점핑파크(마운딩 트램펄린)와 유아용 놀이터를 설치해 다양한 계층이 즐길 수 있는 공원으로 조성하고, 운동 공간 주변으로 조깅을 할 수 있는 순환 산책로도 마련했다.

Namdong-gu renovates Golmaru Neighborhood Park and conducts joint public-private campaign to prevent violence

-Break News Incheon Reporter Park Sang-do

Namdong-gu announced on the 30th that it had renovated Golmaru Neighborhood Park in Mansu 6-dong into a relaxation space that all residents, from children to the elderly, can enjoy and use.

According to Namdong-gu, Golmaru Neighborhood Park is adjacent to Dambang Village Apartment and Dambang Elementary School and is a favorite resting place for residents. However, the facility was very old and there was a risk of safety accidents due to lack of drainage during the rainy season, so a complete facility maintenance was needed.

Accordingly, the district secured a budget of 700 million won as part of the project to improve the environment of the old park in the old downtown, and recently completed construction after completing the detailed design service through a resident briefing session in March of this year.

In addition, the district promoted the project in consideration of the regional characteristics of Mansu 3 District, where the park is located, with many apartments and a variety of age groups living there.

First, we created a street park with exercise equipment that is highly preferred by the elderly and the latest bodyweight exercise equipment for young adults.

In addition, a jumping park (mounding trampoline) and a children's playground where energetic children can play freely were installed to create a park that can be enjoyed by all walks of life, and a circular trail for jogging was also prepared around the exercise area.

In addition, Namdong-gu announced on the 30th that it recently conducted a ‘joint public-private campaign to prevent violence’ in the Rodeo Street area of Guwol-dong to commemorate the 2023 Violence Against Women Week.

According to Namdong-gu, about 30 people participated in this campaign, including the Women-Friendly City Resident Participation Group, Namdong Police Station, Population Health and Welfare Association Sexual Violence Counseling Center, Incheon Disabled Sexual Violence Counseling Center, Incheon Sunflower Center (Children), Incheon Migrant Women's Center Saleo, and volunteers. We carried out a campaign, including distributing promotional materials, under the theme, ‘Creating a safe daily life without violence against women together.’

In addition, we promoted various projects to create a women-friendly city, such as improving awareness of sexual violence and domestic violence crimes and promoting women's rights promotion projects, as well as safety guardian homes for women and children and unmanned safe delivery boxes.

The Week for the Elimination of Violence Against Women (November 25 to December 1 every year) has been operated since 2020 by combining the Week for the Elimination of Sexual Violence and the Week for the Elimination of Domestic Violence in order to raise social interest in the eradication of violence against women in accordance with the Basic Act on the Prevention of Violence Against Women. .
